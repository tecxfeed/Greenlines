Our Proven Recruitment Process

At Greenlines International, we've refined our recruitment process over 32 years to be efficient, transparent, and highly effective. Our methodical approach ensures we connect you with the right talent while minimizing your effort and maximizing your success.

We understand that finding the perfect fit is crucial. Our comprehensive 6-step journey is designed to meticulously vet candidates, streamline all logistics, and provide continuous support, ensuring a seamless and productive partnership from start to finish.

CONNECT US Learn More
01

Job Demand & Analysis

We begin by thoroughly understanding your precise requirements, job specifications, and cultural fit to source candidates who align perfectly with your needs.

02

Rigorous Candidate Shortlisting

Our expert team conducts initial screenings, meticulous resume vetting, and preliminary assessments to identify the most qualified individuals from our global talent pool.

03

Comprehensive Interview &Trade Testing

Shortlisted candidates undergo multi-stage interviews and, where applicable, practical trade tests to verify their skills, experience, and overall suitability.

04

Seamless Medical&Visa Processing

We provide comprehensive assistance with all necessary medical examinations, visa applications, and essential documentation to ensure full compliance and smooth transitions.

05

Efficient Candidate Deployment

Once approved, we manage all logistics, including travel arrangements and pre-departure briefings, ensuring candidates arrive prepared and ready to contribute from day one.

06

Continuous Post-Deployment Support

Our commitment extends beyond placement. We offer ongoing support, facilitate integration, and address any initial challenges, fostering long-term success for both parties.
